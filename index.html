Our Beliefs

Education:
We believe that education is best provided by the free market, achieving greater quality, accountability and efficiency with more diversity of school choice.

Environment:
Clean and healthy environment and sensible use of natural resources, believing that private landowners and conservation groups have a vested interest in maintaining such natural resources.

Healthcare:
We favor a free market health care system without government oversight, approval, regulation. aka no Government heathcare.

Speech and Censorship:
Supports unrestricted freedom of speech and is opposed to any kind of censorship.

2nd Amendment:
Affirms an individual's right recognized by the Second Amendment to keep and bear arms and opposes the prosecution of individuals for exercising their rights of self-defense.
