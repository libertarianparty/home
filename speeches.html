California Rally:
Good afternoon California! America is a beautiful place, and even better are the people. Everyone in the world should get an opportunity to visit this country and experience freedom, how ever they must to legally. The reason why there is so much illegal immigration is because our current system take too long. I will make the application process faster. The expense of living is too high, I will lower taxes. We are in America, the land of opportunity. I will make starting business easier for all Americas. Together we can pull ourselves out of the current mess.
